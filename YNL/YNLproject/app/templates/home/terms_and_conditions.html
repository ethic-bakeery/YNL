Privacy Policy

At ANYL (All Nigerian Youth Congress), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

We collect personal information such as names, email addresses, and contact details when you voluntarily provide them to us. We use this information to communicate with you, process your requests, and improve our services. We do not share your information with third parties except as required by law or to fulfill our services.

Code of Conduct

We are committed to maintaining a respectful and inclusive environment for all members of the ANYL community. We expect all users to adhere to the following Code of Conduct:

  • Respect: Treat others with respect and kindness.
  • Integrity: Engage in honest and ethical behavior.
  • Inclusivity: Foster a welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity.
  • Responsibility: Take responsibility for your actions and their impact on others.

If you have any questions or concerns about our policies, please contact us via our Contact us page. We are here to assist you and address any issues you may have.
